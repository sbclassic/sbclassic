Shipping Policy

Thank you for shopping with SB CLASSIC. We want to make sure your orders arrive safely and on time.

Order Processing

All orders are processed within 1-2 business days from the date of purchase.

Delivery Time

Once your order is processed, delivery typically takes between 2-14 business days, depending on your location within Ghana or internationally.

Shipping Destinations

We currently offer local delivery within Ghana and international shipping to select countries. Please contact us for more details.

Shipping Fees

Shipping fees vary depending on your location and the size of your order. Any applicable shipping costs will be calculated and displayed at checkout.

Tracking Your Order

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with tracking information to monitor your delivery status.
