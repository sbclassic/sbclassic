Shipping Policy – SB CLASSIC

📦 Processing & Delivery Time

At SB CLASSIC, we aim to deliver your orders quickly and efficiently.

  • Processing Time: Orders are processed within 1–2 business days.
  • Delivery Time: Delivery typically takes 2–14 business days, depending on your location.

You will receive a confirmation email or message with tracking information once your order has been shipped.

🌍 Delivery Options

  • Local Delivery (Ghana): Available nationwide via reliable courier services.
  • International Shipping: We currently offer international delivery. Shipping times and fees vary by destination.

🚚 Shipping Fees

Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on your location and the weight/volume of your order.

❗ Delays

While we do our best to meet delivery timelines, delays may occur due to:

  • Courier delays
  • Customs processing (for international orders)
  • Natural events or holidays
